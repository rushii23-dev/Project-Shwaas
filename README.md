# Shwaas

Shwaas is a hyper-local air-pollution app for Delhi. City-wide AQI apps report
one number for the whole city and miss street-level events — a garbage fire,
construction dust, a smog trap at a junction. Shwaas fuses three live signals —
ground sensors, citizen photo reports, and satellite fire data — to catch those
events and flag **hidden hotspots**: places with real evidence of pollution but
no official sensor nearby. It also shows a 24-hour AQI forecast and a live feed
of the worst spots with a suggested action, so any citizen — not just a
municipal office — can see what's actually happening to the air around them.
