# 🌬️ Shwaas

### *You check the weather. Why not the air on your street?*

</div>

---

## What it is

**Shwaas** is a hyper-local air-pollution app for **Delhi**. City-wide AQI apps
report a single number for the whole city — and miss what's actually happening
street by street: a garbage fire two blocks away, construction dust at a
junction, a smog trap where traffic backs up every evening.

Shwaas fuses **three live signals** to see what a city-wide number can't:

| Signal | What it catches |
|:--|:--|
| 🛰️ **Satellites** | NASA thermal-anomaly data — fires no ground sensor sees |
| 📸 **Citizens** | Photos of smoke, dust, or haze, classified live by AI |
| 📟 **Ground sensors** | Real, live station AQI readings across the city |

---

## How it helps

- 🚩 **Flags hidden hotspots** — places with real evidence of pollution but
  **no official sensor nearby** — the exact blind spot city-wide monitoring
  misses.
- 📈 **Forecasts the next 24 hours** of air quality, so a spike can be seen
  coming instead of just noticed.
- 📍 **Shows a live public feed** of the worst spots right now, each with a
  plain-language cause and a suggested action.
- 🙋 **Puts it in everyone's hands** — not a municipal dashboard locked away
  in an office, but a tool any citizen can open and understand at a glance.
